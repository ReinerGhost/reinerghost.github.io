How to earn points?

Each order earns different amount of points, depending on the order’s total. Try ordering less frequent, but heavier bags/loads to collect more points from each order. Every 6 months, your accumulated points will convert into earned Elite Status. If you haven’t earned enough, your level will downgrade to a lower level.
